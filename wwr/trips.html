Our Featured Trips

Watersnake River

Perfect for beginners and families, the Watersnake River offers moderate rapids, beautiful scenery, and experienced guides who ensure safety and fun. This half-day adventure is ideal for those looking to experience whitewater rafting for the first time.

Riviera Maya Rapids

A full-day adrenaline experience featuring Class III and IV rapids. Surrounded by stunning canyon views, this trip challenges teamwork and endurance while delivering unforgettable excitement.

Salto Angel Extreme

Designed for experienced rafters, this advanced expedition includes powerful rapids, technical routes, and breathtaking landscapes. A true test of courage and skill for adventure seekers.
