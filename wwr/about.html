BYU Cougars Rafting Tech

BYU Cougars Rafting Tech exists to combine the spirit of adventure with the power of innovation. Our purpose is to create reliable, cutting-edge rafting technology that empowers people to explore rivers with confidence, safety, and respect for nature. Our mission is to design and deliver high-quality solutions driven by teamwork, integrity, and continuous learning, while honoring a culture of excellence and service. We believe in courage on the water, discipline in our craft, and unity in our community—those are the values that guide every decision we make. Our creed is simple: build with purpose, lead with integrity, and innovate for the greater good. Our motto says it all: “Innovation that Carries You Further.”

History

BYU Cougars Rafting Tech was founded by a group of BYU students and alumni who shared two passions: whitewater rafting and technology-driven problem solving. What began as a small university project focused on improving rafting safety equipment quickly evolved into a dedicated venture as early prototypes proved their value on real rivers. Through collaboration, rigorous testing, and a commitment to excellence, the company grew from a campus-based idea into a trusted name in rafting technology. Today, BYU Cougars Rafting Tech continues to build on its academic roots, blending innovation, adventure, and integrity to support rafters and guides around the world.
